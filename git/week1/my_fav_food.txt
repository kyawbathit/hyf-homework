THE INGREDIENTS FOR GREEN CURRY PASTE
The green color in the curry paste from fresh green chilies, hence the name green curry. Here are the complete list of ingredients:

1. Fresh green chilies
2. Cilantro roots or stems (not the leaves
3. Garlic
4. Shallots
5. Lemongrass
6. Galangal
7. Coriander
8. Cumin
9. Shrimp paste
10. Kaffir lime skin and leaves
