Tomyum Soup
1. 12 to 14 medium shrimp (or 8 to 10 large, raw, shells left on or removed)
2. 6 cups chicken stock.
3. 1 stalk lemongrass (lower 1/3, finely minced)
4. 4 cloves garlic (minced)
5. 3 tablespoons onion (minced)
6. 6 teaspoons galangal (or ginger)
7. 3 leaves kaffir lime (or bay leaves)
8. 1 teaspoon lime juice (or lemon juice)
